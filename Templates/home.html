Traffic congestion costs 1.47 lakh crore per annum for India according a study in 2018 by Boston Consulting Group (BCG). The same is the situation in most of the developed hubs across the world. Traffic issues cause so much inconvenience and frustration and hence need to be addressed. While public transport is the most natural solution to this problem , not all cities have a sophisticated and well developed public transport system .

Hence most of the metropolitian citizens have now turned towards carpooling and other forms of shared transport. BYKE comes into picture right in this space!!

BYKE is a ride sharing platform specifically designed for bike rides , whereby we try to solve the problem of short commute and reduce the dependency on public transport systems and autos . We connect drivers and passengers travelling to the same destination, allowing them to share a ride .

How It Works
1. REGISTER

Register yourself on BYKE to avail the service.


2. LOGIN

Login into your account as a driver if you wish to offer a ride or as a passenger if you wish to take a ride.

3. ENTER RIDE DETAILS

Enter starting location , destination , date and time of your travel


4 . SEE MATCHING RIDES

You can now see all matching rides along with the fare which the passenger must pay to the driver at the end of the ride.Happy Journey !!
